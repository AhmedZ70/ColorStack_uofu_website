About Us

ColorStack at the University of Utah is a community dedicated to empowering Black and Latinx computer science students. We provide academic support, mentorship, and career development opportunities.

Our chapter hosts workshops, networking events, and study sessions, fostering an inclusive environment where students can connect, learn, and succeed together.
